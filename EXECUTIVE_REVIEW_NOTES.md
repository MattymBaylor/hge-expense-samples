# HGE AI Expense Overview — Executive Review Notes

## Recommended Presentation Updates

### 1. Replace "Business justified"
Recommended replacement terminology:
- Operational allocation
- Operationally allocated
- Operational purpose
- Operational spend allocation

Recommended final choice:
## Operational allocation

This creates a cleaner accounting and executive-review tone.

---

### 2. Limit "Top Vendors" chart to Top 10
Current chart contains too many vendors and reduces readability.

Recommended:
## Top 10 Platforms by Operational Spend

Group remaining vendors into:
- Other Production
- Other R&D
- Reclassified / Non-operational

This improves:
- executive readability
- accounting review
- audit clarity
- operational storytelling

---

### 3. Add operational framing summary
Recommended language:

> This report summarizes AI, infrastructure, automation, hosting, and operational software expenses incurred during HGE's deployment of AI-assisted workflow, marketing, lead management, customer communication, and automation systems from January–April 2026.

> Expenses were categorized into Production Infrastructure, Research & Development, Operational Software, and Reclassified/Non-Operational categories.

> Personal and non-operational expenses were excluded where applicable.

---

### 4. Improve vendor descriptions
Current wording occasionally sounds defensive or experimental.

Recommended tone:
- operational
- infrastructure-oriented
- deployment-focused
- workflow-oriented

Example:

Instead of:
> AI image gen evaluated vs Midjourney & Firefly

Use:
> AI image generation platform utilized for marketing creative development and production content workflows.

---

### 5. Accounting-review recommendations
Recommended additional filters:
- Category
- Month
- Operational vs R&D
- Transitional account review
- Excluded/reclassified
- Amount TBD

Recommended additional flags:
- Transitional account
- Pending receipt confirmation
- Reclassified expense
- Excluded from operational allocation

---

### 6. Biggest accounting concern
The monthly "Business justified" totals currently exceed monthly statement totals.

These should either:
- be relabeled as cumulative operational allocation
OR
- recalculated before final review.

This is the single biggest presentation risk in the current report.

---

### 7. Strongest strategic narrative
The strongest framing is not:
> experimenting with AI tools

The strongest framing is:
> infrastructure modernization and operational automation deployment

The current spend profile strongly supports this positioning.
